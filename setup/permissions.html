Permissions & Privacy

This extension requires certain permissions to function properly. These are:

  • Access to visited URLs: To scan the URLs you visit for malicious content.
  • Web request monitoring: To monitor your browsing activity safely and securely for potential threats.
  • Local storage access: To store the API key and caching scan results for better performance.

We only send URLs to the VirusTotal API for analysis, and do not share your data with anyone else. Your privacy is our utmost priority.
